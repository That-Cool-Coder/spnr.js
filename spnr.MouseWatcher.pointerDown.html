spnr.MouseWatcher.pointerDown

Reference contents

Syntax:

spnr.MouseWatcher.pointerDown

Example:

var canJump = watcher.pointerDown

Description:

Boolean that states whether the mouse is down or the screen is touched in watcher.elem. Note: this tracks both mouse and touchscreen. For individual listeners, see spnr.MouseWatcher.mouseDown and spnr.MouseWatcher.touchDown.

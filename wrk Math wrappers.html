spnr Math wrappers

Reference contents

Syntax:

spnr Math wrappers

Example:

var a = spnr.PI

Description:

There is a spnr wrapper around every js Math function/value, EXCEPT for the values that are defined by spnr. An example of a Math method overwritten by spnr is spnr.round, which is not identical to the Math variant.

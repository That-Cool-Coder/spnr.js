spnr.MouseWatcher.onTouchEnd

Reference contents

Syntax:

spnr.MouseWatcher.onTouchEnd

Example:

watcher.onTouchEnd.add(jump)

Description:

A spnr.FunctionGroup that is called when a touchscreen tap ends on watcher.elem. Is not called by mouse clicks - for that see spnr.MouseWatcher.onPointerUp or spnr.MouseWatcher.onMouseUp.
